1. Long-term isolation and archaic introgression shape functional genetic variation in Near Oceania

⭐ 👍 👎 已读
Patrick F. Reilly、Stephen Rong、Daniela Tejada-Martinez、Samantha L. Miller 等 15 人 · DOI: 10.1126/science.adr6749

一句话

这篇论文基于177个近大洋洲高覆盖度全基因组，并结合1284个全球基因组数据，分析了近大洋洲人群在岛屿间与岛内的遗传分化、长期隔离、强瓶颈及古人类渗入历史。摘要指出作者重建了约18.97亿碱基对的古老基因组片段，识别出来自三支类丹尼索瓦群体的渗入信号，并通过大规模平行报告实验检测到3127个高频渗入来源的表达调控变异，涉及TRPS1以及JAK1、GBP2、OAS1等功能基因。

贡献

可能的核心贡献包括：1）为长期在基因组学中代表性不足的近大洋洲人群提供了177例高覆盖全基因组资源，显著补充区域性人类遗传数据；2）在群体历史层面，具体揭示了近大洋洲不同岛屿群体之间和群体内部的显著遗传差异，并将其与长期隔离和强人口瓶颈联系起来；3）在古人类渗入层面，重建了大规模古老序列，其中包含约8.319亿碱基对丹尼索瓦序列，并提出近大洋洲存在至少三支类丹尼索瓦来源的渗入证据；4）在适应性进化层面，指出TRPS1这一骨骼发育基因存在适应性丹尼索瓦渗入信号，并将其与中非雨林狩猎采集人群和厄瓜多尔高地人群中的选择现象进行比较；5）在功能验证层面，使用massively parallel reporter assay这一具体实验方法，筛出3127个高频、渗入来源且可调节表达的变异，并发现其功能影响富集于干扰素-γ信号通路相关基因，如JAK1、GBP2和OAS1。

Near Oceanic populations harbor substantial cultural, phenotypic, and genetic diversity yet are drastically underrepresented in human genomics. We generated 177 high-coverage Near Oceanian whole genomes and analyzed them alongside 1284 worldwide genomes, revealing major distinctions among and within islands, including long-term isolation and strong population bottlenecks. We reconstructed 1.897 billion base pairs of the archaic genome, including 831.9 million base pairs of Denisovan sequence, and found evidence for introgression from three Denisovan-like groups in Near Oceanians and adaptive Denisovan introgression at TRPS1 , a skeletal development gene also under selection in central African rainforest hunter-gatherers and highland Ecuadorians. We then performed a massively parallel reporter assay and discovered 3127 high-frequency introgressed expression-modulating variants, finding an enrichment of functional impacts on genes in the interferon-γ signaling pathway including JAK1 , GBP2 , and OAS1 .

贡献

核心贡献在于提出并初步证实了一种“冰冻圈变化向深海生态传导”的具体机制：含碎屑冰山将落石输送到弗拉姆海峡海底，从而增加深海硬底基质并可能重塑底栖生物多样性。具体而言，论文的可能贡献包括：1）在对象层面，将研究焦点从海冰和冰川前缘扩展到远距离下游深海海底栖息地；2）在证据层面，结合海底落石观测与四十年船载冰山出现记录，指出冰山出现频率在21世纪初发生突增，并与东北格陵兰出口冰川失稳时间相吻合；3）在来源归因层面，通过回溯分析将冰山主要联系到东北格陵兰和俄罗斯高北极的主要出口冰川，同时明确俄方区域因卫星覆盖稀疏而时间归因仍有限；4）在机制层面，用模型敏感性试验表明，除崩解增强外，更动态的海冰覆盖也会促进冰川冰下游输运，放大冰山交通及其生态效应。

Abstract The Arctic is undergoing rapid warming, resulting in retreating sea ice and glaciers 1 , yet how cryospheric changes propagate into the deep ocean remains poorly understood 2 . Here we identify a climate-driven mechanism linking accelerating glacier disintegration to an increase in deep-sea hard-bottom habitats far beyond calving fronts. Seafloor observations in Fram Strait show a localized increase in the density and patchiness of dropstones delivered by debris-laden icebergs. At the same time, four decades of shipboard records show that the occurrence of icebergs increased abruptly in the early 2000s. Backtracking links these icebergs to the main outlet glaciers in northeast Greenland and the Russian High Arctic. In northeast Greenland, the timing of glacier destabilization coincides with this rise, whereas sparse satellite coverage in the Russian sector limits temporal attribution despite indications of enhanced glacier activity. A model sensitivity study shows that, apart from intensified calving, a more dynamic sea ice cover enhances downstream transport of glacial ice. Along these pathways, increased iceberg activity could reshape deep-sea habitats through enhanced melt and associated lithogenic input, and elevate navigational hazards as maritime traffic expands in the Arctic. Although modest compared with the iceberg discharges of Pleistocene Heinrich events, this mechanism provides a modern analogue of long-range cryospheric influence on the seafloor in a warming climate.

4. Patterns of brain-wide associations reflect socioeconomics

⭐ 👍 👎 已读
Scott Marek、Meghan Rose Donohue、Nicole R. Karcher、Caroline P. Hoyniak 等 36 人 · DOI: 10.1126/science.aee6213
ai 1 environmental 1 environment 1
一句话

该研究基于儿童样本，将649个环境与行为变量分别与全脑变异建立脑广泛关联图谱（BWAS），并比较这些图谱之间及其与神经生物学参考模式的关系。摘要显示，社会经济地位（SES）产生了最强的全脑关联，其空间模式主要落在运动与感觉区域而非认知区域；这一共同模式也出现在包括IQ在内的多种变量上，且在校正SES后脑-IQ关联明显减弱并失去该运动/感觉模式。

贡献

可能的核心贡献包括：1）在儿童脑影像场景下系统映射并横向比较649个变量的BWAS图谱，而非仅考察单一行为或环境因素；2）提出存在一个跨变量共享的“共同BWAS脑模式”，且该模式最能反映儿童的社会经济条件，具体表现为对运动和感觉系统的更强关联；3）通过SES校正分析指出，部分脑-IQ关联可能被社会经济相关变异显著解释，从而改变对认知相关脑关联的解释框架；4）通过在高SES子样本训练时脑-IQ关联泛化不佳这一现象，提示相关模型可能受SES分布影响，具有样本构成依赖性；5）摘要进一步提出睡眠剥夺和压力可能是SES影响儿童脑变异的潜在途径，但基于现有信息这更接近机制假设而非已完整证实的结论。

Previous brain-wide association studies (BWAS) have linked specific environmental and behavioral variables to brain variability. In this work, we mapped 649 variables to children’s brains and compared the resultant BWAS maps with each other and with neurobiological reference patterns. Socioeconomic status (SES) showed the strongest brain-wide associations. The SES associations were strongest in motor and sensory but not cognitive regions, a pattern shared across many BWAS maps, including intelligence quotient (IQ). A single, common BWAS brain pattern existed across variables that was most reflective of a child’s socioeconomics. Adjusting for SES weakened brain-IQ associations, eliminating the BWAS motor and sensory pattern. Brain-with-IQ associations also did not generalize when trained on higher-SES subsamples. Thus, children’s brains vary the most with SES, potentially through SES-dependent sleep deprivation and stress.
